About me

Nikki X

My name is Nikki Xaysanasy and I'm an aspiring Web Developer. My favorite thing about coding is looking at neatly organized code. My dream is to become a Full-Stack JavaScript Developer and work on many different apps and websites.

I'm most familiar with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. I'm currently learning Angular and NativeScript and love discovering new technologies and framework in the web development world. I use many different learning platforms such as Udacity, Lynda, Coursera and many more.

Aside from web developing and working on my apps, I like to read and write. My passion was to be a detective but I decided to write about one instead. I'm also a performer and in many different music projects including a pop punk/emo band and as a Rapper. I used to DJ professionally and taught a couple folks in NWA that are still continuing to DJ. I performed live in different states and even performed overseas. I still perform and write music on my acoustic guitar. And it's not something that I will be giving up anytime soon.
