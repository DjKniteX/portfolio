Who I Am

My name is Nikki Xaysanasy and before Web Development I never really wanted to do anything. I dabbled with Wordpress making personal sites but it wans't until early 2017 where I was introduced to the world of Web Development by a good friend of mine. I self-studied for 6 months while taking on small free freelance projects and then joined a Front-End Development Course at the University of Arkansas where I studied for 6 additional months in an educational setting. I worked shortly as a Web Developer in Arkansas before moving to California for better job opportunities.

It's 2018. I'm in Los Angeles and I'm still learning. Welcome to my portfolio and I hope you enjoy your stay.
